About

Wolf Cola is an up and coming soon-to-be major player in the international soda market. Based out of Philadelphia Pennsylvania, Wolf Cola has the strong reputation as a Cola that is beloved by a diverse group of people. Young and old, big and small, the people of Philadelphia, and the world alike are all falling in love with the sweet smooth taste of Wolf Cola.

Our CEO, Frank Reynolds

This delicious cola is one of the many drinks offered by the famous beverage company, Franks Fluids LLC. Along with Caribbean Paradise, and Fight Milk, Wolf Cola makes for a well-rounded slate of drinks that are sure to leave your thirst for deliciousness quenched.
